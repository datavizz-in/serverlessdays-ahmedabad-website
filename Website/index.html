What is ServerlessDays?
Aim of this event is to instil desire to learn and grow ourselves with the “serverless” architecture. For this we have invited expert & experienced speakers who will share their knowledge about serverless.
Why to attend?
ServerlessDays Ahmedabad is a community organised event focusing on Serverless technologies and the changing face of application development. We aim to make it an inclusive conference welcoming people from diverse backgrounds.
