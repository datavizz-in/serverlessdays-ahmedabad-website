Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend Serverless Days Ahmedabad 2023?
There are many reasons. It is Global but local Serverless Community event. Please refer our goal of Serverless Ahmedabad Community.
What is your cancellation policy?
Registration charges are non refundable and non transferable.
What is included in event registration?
Registration gives you access to the keynote, all technical sessions, the technology booth and closing reception. Meals offered include lunch and snacks.
Can I stop by onsite without being registered?
No! Due to the high demand for Serverless Days Ahmedabad 2023, we do not grant access to walk-ins with no upfront registration at the event venue. Thank you for your understanding!
Where is my confirmation email?
If you have registered, you should receive a confirmation email and SMS shortly after registering. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please check your spam folder. Once you have checked your spam folder, if you still haven't received your confirmation email, please contact Serverless Ahmedabad Community.
Will the sessions be in English/Hindi/Gujarati language?
All sessions will be in English since our audience is coming from across the country and we want to make sure everybody can follow.
Do you have any travel policy for the speakers?
No! We do not provide travel assistance to the speaker. It is the speaker's responsibility to cover their own travel expenses to speak at the event.
How can I contact the event organizers?
If you have questions that have not been answered in the FAQ above, please contact Serverless Ahmedabad Community.
